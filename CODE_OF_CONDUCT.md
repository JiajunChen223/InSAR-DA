# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep technical discussions focused on evidence, reproducibility, and maintainability.
- Assume good intent while being precise about problems and risks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, intimidation, or publishing others' private information are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet this standard.
